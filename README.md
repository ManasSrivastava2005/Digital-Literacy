## 📌 Digital Literacy Project – Overview

This repository contains my **Digital Literacy Project** completed as part of the **CSE0001 course** at VIT Bhopal. The project focuses on developing essential digital skills required for academic, professional, and online environments.

The main objective of this project was to build a **comprehensive digital portfolio** showcasing knowledge of digital awareness, online security, professional communication, and modern collaboration tools.

---

## 🎯 Project Highlights

This project is divided into five key tasks:

* **Task 1: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic**
  Created a visually engaging infographic using Canva to explain key concepts like safe internet practices, professional presence, and digital tools.

* **Task 2: Student Digital Portfolio Development**
  Built and organized professional profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Stack Overflow to establish a strong online presence.

* **Task 3: Coding & Collaboration Tools**
  Solved coding problems on HackerRank and created a Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz using Google Forms with response tracking.

* **Task 4: Professional Email & Etiquette**
  Drafted formal emails for academic and career purposes, focusing on clarity, structure, and professional tone.

* **Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness & Prevention**
  Researched cyber threats like phishing and UPI fraud, and explored preventive measures including 2FA and safe browsing practices.

---

## 💡 Key Learnings

* Importance of maintaining a **professional digital identity**
* Effective use of **online collaboration tools**
* Awareness of **cybersecurity threats and prevention**
* Writing **professional emails and communication**
* Building a **portfolio for future career opportunities**
